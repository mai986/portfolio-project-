How I Work

I follow a clear, organised process to ensure every project runs smoothly from start to finish. My goal is always to deliver high-quality websites that meet client needs while providing a positive, collaborative experience.

My Process:

  1. Initial Consultation: I start by discussing the client’s goals, target audience, and design preferences. This helps define the project direction.
  2. Planning and Research: I research similar websites, analyse competitors, and create a basic project plan, including site structure and key features.
  3. Design and Wireframing: I create wireframes or mock-ups to map out page layouts, ensuring the site will be clear, user-friendly, and visually appealing.
  4. Development: I build the website using clean, semantic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, always keeping responsiveness, SEO, and accessibility in mind.
  5. Testing and Review: I test the website across different devices and browsers to ensure everything works correctly. I also gather client feedback for final adjustments.
  6. Launch and Handover: Once approved, I launch the site and provide basic guidance on maintenance or future updates if needed.

Throughout the project, I value clear communication, attention to detail, and delivering work on time and to a high standard.
